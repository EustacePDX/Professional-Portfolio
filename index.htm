Welcome

Hello, my name is Kyle Kempton. Welcome to my professional portfolio website!

I am a recent honors graduate from Eckerd College with a degree in computer science and a 3.56 GPA.

During my academic work I was involved with several projects. I acted as a research assistant on a bioinformatics project verifying calculations of fractal dimensions made on the GPU using C++ and CUDA code. I have developed multiple websites for community clients as a way to interact with their customers using HTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL queries. I created an extension of the Java API libraries to make Euclidean geometric calculations more efficient. I have written an iPhone application in Objective C for iOS designed to show mastery of GUI concepts using NStimers. I created a short animation demonstrating proficiency with complex graphics concepts rendered at 600 by 800 resolution at 24 FPS. And lastly I programmed a multiclass user input game demonstrating data structures mastery and using the Java Swing libraries.

Professionally I have IT experience working as a lab assistant at the Advanced Scientific Computing Lab of Eckerd College from 2012-2015. And I have experience in the domestic workplace as an assistant to an autistic adult from 2009-present.

Perhaps most importantly though I posess a strong passion for computer science. I enjoy the aspect of the puzzle that working with computers presents. I have learned how to be patient and persistant with them while also being productive. And I crave the sense of accomplishment that finally getting the computer to act as I want it to act provides.

If my credentials are of interest to you, please feel free to peruse this website at your leisure. Also you can contact me at kk@kylekempton.com.
